PRIVACY POLICY

OrgPulse is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose your data when you interact with our platform.

1. Information We Collect:
When you register as a donor or patient — either individually or through a medical institute — we collect necessary information including your name, contact details, health-related information, and your association with a medical institution (if applicable).

2. Purpose of Data Collection:
The data you provide is used exclusively for:

  • Matching organ donors with potential recipients
  • Verification by associated medical institutes
  • Facilitating communication between relevant parties
  • Statistical analysis to improve our services (only using anonymized data)

3. Data Security:
Your information is stored securely on our servers with appropriate encryption and access control. Only authorized personnel involved in donor-patient coordination or platform management can access your information.

4. Data Sharing:
We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When legally required by law enforcement or court orders
  • With verified medical institutions involved in the organ donation process
Your explicit consent will be sought wherever applicable.

5. Email and Communication:
If you contact us or register with OrgPulse, your email may be used to respond to queries or send updates related to your registration. We do not send marketing emails or share your email with external entities.

6. Cookies:
OrgPulse does not use cookies to store personal information or track user behavior. Your browsing session is private and secure.

7. Your Rights:
You may:

  • Request to view the data we hold about you
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Withdraw your information from the platform

8. Policy Updates:
We may revise this policy from time to time. Any changes will be updated on this page with an appropriate revision date.

By using OrgPulse, you acknowledge and accept this Privacy Policy.
