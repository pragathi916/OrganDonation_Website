TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Welcome to OrgPulse, a platform dedicated to supporting organ donation by enabling individuals and medical institutes to register as donors or patients and to connect with those in need.

1. Personal Information Collection:
You may browse the OrgPulse website without revealing any personal information. However, to register as a donor or patient, either individually or via a medical institute, certain personal and medical information is required. This data is collected solely to support your intended use of the platform and ensure effective donor-patient matching.

2. Usage Logs:
For statistical and security purposes, the website logs standard information such as your IP address, domain name, browser type, date and time of access, pages visited, documents downloaded, and the referring site. These logs are not used to identify individual users unless required by law.

3. Cookies:
OrgPulse does not use cookies to store personal data. Your session information is handled securely and cleared once you leave the site.

4. Email and Communication:
Your email address will be recorded only if you voluntarily send us a message or register. It will be used only for communication relevant to your request or registration. We do not share your email address with third parties without your consent, and we do not send promotional emails.
